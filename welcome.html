Getting Started with qTube

Easliy add currently playing video

When you are on a Youtube video page, notice a [Add to Queue] button below the video. Clicking on the button will add the video to the current queue. In case it is the first video, it will automatically create a new queue and add the video in the queue.
Note: There will only be one queue playing in only one tab. Adding video from other tabs/windows will add them to the same queue.

Add From Recommendations

You can easily add videos from the set of recommended/related videos on a video page. You can eighter right click the video and add it from qTube right click menu or you can mouse over the video and click the qTube icon. (see screenshot).

qTube UI

qTube uses the same UI used by Youtube playlist with which you are probably already familiar with. When you create a queue, the app will autmatically load the UI.

Right Click Menu

qTube comes with a right click menu which you can use to add/remove videos from the queue. Right click on any youtube video link on any page and you will see the qTube option for adding/removing videos.

Control from anywhere

qTube comes with preset keyboard shortcuts, but please make sure that these are available i.e. not being used by any other extension or app previously. You can see/change the shortcuts by visiting chrome://extensions in your browser tab and scroll to the bottom. Currently qTube supports shortcuts for play/pause video and play next/previous video from the queue.

Remember the Queue is temporary. If you close the main tab playing the queue or go to a different address in the tab, the queue will be destroyed. However, there is a delay in destroying the queue, if you have accidently closed the tab or cliked on a link, you can come back to the queue within 5-10 sec or otherwise the queue will be destroyed. :).
